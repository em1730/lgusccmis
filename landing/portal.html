VAMOS MOBILE

VAMOS MOBILE is the official contact tracing mobile application of San Carlos City, Negros Occidental, Philippines.

It is designed to register entities, such as individual, juridical and transportation, for the monitoring of their activities for easier and faster contact tracing.

About Vamos Mobile

Introducing the official contact tracing mobile app of San Carlos City.

Keeping track of citizens’ whereabouts is important in fighting against coronavirus. So whenever you go out in public places such as malls, markets, offices, churches, schools, government offices and other buildings or establishments, please make sure to use your VAMOS mobile application or ID to record your activities. VAMOS helps the government track people in close contact with suspected patients. Here are some of the objectives in using the app:

  • To keep an eye on citizens’ whereabouts;
  • To ensure people follow quarantine protocols;
  • To avoid long queues;
  • To help students, workers and residents stay safe;
  • To make it safer for customers to dine-in;
  • To enlighten people on their likelihood of infection;
  • To collect medical information;
  • To register visitors’ from neighboring towns and cities;
  • To track the movement of residents;
  • To encourage people to use digital tools.
